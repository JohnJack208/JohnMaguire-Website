Born in Boston, MA in 1990, the 3rd child of three. During the recession, his family moved to SoCal while John was in his infancy. With a rough and lonely upbringing, Maguire hid in fantasy and impossibility, were the tales and advantures of far away lands and distant stars captivated him. John's earliest memories have always been of Fiction. John is obsessed with (and fascinated by) Storytellling. Driven to entertain, educate, and inspire, Maguire works to earn a degree and contribute a tale or two of his own.

Having suffered through public school, John shyed away from further education, drifting from one dead-end job to another. However, after the death of his father, Maguire felt compelled to try and achieve a childhood dream, and, with a little luck, enrolled at the University of California, Riverside.

John ("Jack" to all the friends he doesn't have) hopes to write for a living, mostly in Comics, but anything that keeps him off the streets is more than welcome. At this time, he is unpublished and still very much a student, with no first-hand experience or real ability. John is very excited about this promising future in cleaning tables and washing dishes.
